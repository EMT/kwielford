Some things you can ask me to do…

say
---

Make me print something. Eg.:

/kwiz say PRINT ME!

Work in progress stuff. We want the syntax to be as quick and intuitive as possible, so please improve on this!

remind me
---------

/kwiz remind me at 2pm to do that thing
/kwiz remind me on thursday at 9am to do that other thing
/kwiz remind me at 3pm on 25 aug to do that thing in the distant future

todo
----

Show me my todo list:
/kwiz todo

Add an item to a todo list
/kwiz todo This is something I have to do

As above but tag with a project
/kwiz todo This is something I have to do [t:Project Title]
